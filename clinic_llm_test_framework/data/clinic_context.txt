Springfield Medical Center is located at 123 Health St., Springfield. Our main campus houses a general practice, specialist clinics, and a pharmacy.

The clinic is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The clinic is closed on Sundays and public holidays.

We accept most major insurance plans including BlueCross, Medicare, and Medicaid. Please bring your insurance card to your appointment.

To schedule an appointment, patients can call our front desk at (555) 123-4567 or use our online portal. For rescheduling or cancellations, please give us at least 24 hours notice.

For blood tests and fasting labs, please arrive at least 15 minutes early and do not eat for 8 hours prior.
